About Me

I am a professional photographer that specializes in birds and fish. I began my love for these creatures way back in middle school when our class went to the zoo to see the animals. We went in a bird cage and I got a little lost along the way. My teacher wasn't very responsible so I ended up getting stuck in the bird cage overnight. At first I was afraid of the birds, but I soon learned to make friends with them. They became my best friends throughout the years that I spent in that cage. They taught me many useful things, including how to appreciate fish, my other great passion. I love looking at fish and taking their pictures. I believe that everyone should be able to appreciate their beauty.

I photograph pictures of fish and birds. I also am capable of taking pictures of other things too, I guess.
